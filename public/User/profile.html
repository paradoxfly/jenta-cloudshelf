About Project

Jenta Cloud-Shelf E-library is an open, editable library catalog with its population focus being the people of Jenta, Jos. The library allows its users to upload books to its database, with the permission of its administrators. This allows for the availabiity of a large array of books.

Users can choose to read the books in the library environment, or download it to their local environment.
